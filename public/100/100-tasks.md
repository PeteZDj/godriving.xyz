# The 100 — GoDriving

> **GoDriving** · godriving.xyz
>
> GoDriving teaches the highway code by playing — road-sign games, quizzes and an arcade driving game — and connects new drivers with trusted local driving schools, starting in Kenya. Here is every one of the 100 startup tasks, answered for GoDriving, with our status and next move.

**Progress snapshot:** ✅ 22 done · 🟡 51 in progress · ⬜ 27 to do (of 100).

Framework: *The 100 Tasks Startup System* by Martin Bell — Setup (1–25) · Launch (26–69) · Scale (70–100). The live, interactive checklist is at **https://godriving.xyz/100**.
